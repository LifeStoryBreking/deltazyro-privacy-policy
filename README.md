## Introduction

DeltaZyro Screen Recorder is a simple browser extension for recording your screen, browser tabs, and windows with optional audio.

## What Data Do We Collect?

**We collect ZERO personal data.**

- We do NOT collect your name, email, or personal information
- We do NOT track your browsing history
- We do NOT collect your recordings or any recording metadata
- We do NOT use analytics or tracking
- We do NOT collect location data

## How Does It Work?

Everything happens **on your computer only**:
- You click "Start Recording"
- Your device captures the screen/tab/window
- Recording stays on your device
- When you click "Stop", you download the MP4 file
- That's it!

## What Are These Permissions?

The extension needs these permissions to work:

**Screen Capture Permission:**
- Allows you to record your desktop or specific windows
- Only used when YOU click "Start Recording"

**Tab Capture Permission:**
- Allows you to record your browser tab
- Only used when you choose "Record This Tab"

**Microphone Permission:**
- Captures your voice or microphone input
- Only used if you enable audio recording

**Storage Permission:**
- Saves your MP4 files to your computer
- Downloads go to your "Downloads" folder

## Data Sharing

We do NOT share any data with:
- Google
- Third-party companies
- Analytics services
- Ad networks
- Anyone else

Your recordings are YOURS and stay on your device.

## Security

- No cloud storage of any kind
- No internet connection needed
- No accounts or login required
- No tracking or monitoring
- Everything is local and private
